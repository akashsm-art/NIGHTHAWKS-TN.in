Frequently Asked Questions

Go to the Dashboard, find an available match, click "Register", enter your 4 player names (Player 1 is the IGL), and submit. You'll be auto-assigned to a batch with available slots.
Each match has 3 batches with different time slots. Batch 1: 5-6 PM, Batch 2: 6-7 PM, Batch 3: 7-9 PM. Each batch holds up to 12 teams. You're automatically assigned to the first batch with open slots.
The admin will set the Room ID and Password before the match starts. You can find them in the "My Matches" section once they're published. Make sure to check before your batch time.
The admin controls when registration opens and closes. Registration also auto-clears at 9:00 PM daily. If registration is closed, you'll see a message on the Dashboard. Try again when it reopens.
Use the "Player Support" button (💬) on the Dashboard, or fill out the contact form on this page with "Report a Player" as the subject. Include the player's name, team, and details of the violation.
On the login page, click "Forgot password?" and enter your registered mobile number. You'll receive an OTP to verify your identity, then you can set a new password.
